             Long ago              I heard

                 of Lake      Dongting,

         now I climb Yueyang Tower: Wu and Chu

slope off to south and          east,Heaven and Earth day

and night float on these        waters.Of kinfolk, friends,

not one word,old, sickly,       in my solitary boat,and

      north of the barrier mountains the fighting

                      goes on--

                  as I lean on the

              railing, tears stream down.
